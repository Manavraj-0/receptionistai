Call Recordings & Data

Recording of Calls

All calls handled by the Agent are recorded and transcribed by default as part of the Service. Call recordings and transcripts are stored on infrastructure operated by Retell AI, Inc. ("Retell"), DaizyAI's voice AI sub-processor.

Client's obligation: The Client is responsible as Data Controller for ensuring that their end-customers are appropriately informed that calls may be recorded and may be handled by an AI system. DaizyAI's inbound Agents do not proactively announce themselves as AI at the start of a call, but will truthfully confirm they are AI if a caller directly asks. The Client must ensure their own privacy notice accurately describes AI voice agent call handling and recording, so that callers have been appropriately informed before calling.

Access to Recordings

The Client may access their call recordings and transcripts via the DaizyAI reporting dashboard for the duration of the retention period. DaizyAI may also access recordings for quality assurance, Agent optimisation, and client reporting purposes. No other party is permitted to access recordings without the Client's consent or a legal obligation.

Retention

Call recordings and transcripts are retained for a default period of 12 months from the call date. Clients may request a shorter retention period or deletion of specific recordings at any time. On termination of the Agreement, all caller data will be deleted within 30 days unless the Client requests earlier deletion or we are required by law to retain it.

Deletion Requests

Deletion requests must be submitted in writing to daizyaiinfo@gmail.com. We will action deletion from Retell AI's infrastructure within 14 days of a verified request. Confirmation of deletion will be provided in writing.

Client Responsibility for Caller Data

The Client is the Data Controller for personal data relating to their callers. DaizyAI acts as Data Processor. Template Reuse & Intellectual Property in Agents

Vertical Templates

DaizyAI builds and maintains a library of Vertical Templates — anonymised, generalised configuration frameworks for each sector we serve. DaizyAI may use a Vertical Template as the starting point for the Client's Agent, and may use learnings from the Client's Agent deployment to improve Vertical Templates for use in future deployments.

What we use and what we do not: Vertical Templates contain only generalised, anonymised configuration logic (scripts, decision flows, qualifying question formats). They never contain the Client's business name, branding, or any personal data relating to the Client's callers.

Specifically, DaizyAI will never incorporate into any Vertical Template:

  • The Client's business name, trading name, or branding
  • The names, phone numbers, or any other personal data of the Client's callers
  • Call recordings (audio or transcript) attributable to the Client
  • CRM credentials, integration configurations, or webhook data
  • Commercially sensitive information specific to the Client's business
