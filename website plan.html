AI Receptionist Website Plan

Conversion-Focused SaaS Landing Page Structure

🎯 Objective

Your website has one goal:

Convert visitors into demo calls or leads

🧠 Core Flow

Attention → Trust → Demo → Problem → Solution → Proof → Action

🚀 1. Hero Section

  • Clear headline (outcome-focused)
  • Subheadline explaining value
  • Primary CTA (Try Demo / Book Call)
  • Visual or call preview

🤝 2. Trust Section

  • "Used by X businesses"
  • Logos (future)
  • Quick testimonial

☎️ 3. Live Demo

  • Call number
  • "Try it yourself"
  • Optional recorded calls

🚨 4. Problem Section

  • Missed calls = lost revenue
  • Busy staff
  • Lost leads

✅ 5. Solution Section

  • AI answers instantly
  • Handles conversations
  • Captures leads

⚙️ 6. How It Works

  • Customer calls
  • AI interacts
  • You get results

🧩 7. Features

  • 24/7 answering
  • Custom scripts
  • Appointment booking
  • Analytics

🏢 8. Use Cases

  • Clinics
  • Immigration firms
  • Real estate
  • Local businesses

⭐ 9. Social Proof

  • Testimonials
  • Results (before/after)

💰 10. Pricing

  • Custom pricing model
  • Starting from £X
  • CTA: Get Quote

❓ 11. FAQ

  • How minutes are calculated
  • Customization options
  • Overage pricing
  • Setup process

🚀 12. Final CTA

"Ready to never miss a call again?"

📞 13. Footer

  • Contact info
  • WhatsApp
  • Legal links

⚠️ Key Principles

  • Keep it simple
  • Focus on outcomes, not tech
  • One clear CTA
  • Demo is your biggest asset
